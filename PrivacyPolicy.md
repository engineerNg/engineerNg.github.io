# MB Master Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 2, 2026

---

### 1. Overview

MB Master is a habit tracking application ("the App"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

### 2. Information We Collect

#### 2.1 Information You Provide

When you use the App, we may collect the following information:

- **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect:
  - Email address (for email/password signup)
  - Display name
  - Profile photo URL
- **App Settings:** Language preference, theme settings
- **Feedback:** Any feedback or requests you submit through the App

#### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data:** App usage events and interactions for analytics purposes
- **Device Identifier:** Anonymous user identifier (UUID) for guest users
- **Habit Records:** Tags and categories you create, daily check-in records, and tracking history

#### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

When you sign in with a third-party service, we receive only the information you authorize us to access:

- **Google Sign-In:** Name, email address, and profile photo (if shared)
- **Sign in with Apple:** Name and email address (if shared)

### 3. How We Use Your Information

We collect and use your information for the following purposes:

- **Provide Core Features:** Enable habit tracking, daily check-ins, and data visualization
- **Data Synchronization:** Sync your habit records across your devices via Firebase
- **Account Management:** Process authentication and account-related requests
- **Analytics & Improvement:** Analyze usage patterns to improve app performance and user experience
- **Customer Support:** Respond to your feedback, questions, and requests
- **Security:** Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats

### 4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Provider |
|---------|---------|----------|
| Firebase Authentication | User authentication and account management | Google LLC |
| Firebase Analytics | App usage analytics and crash reporting | Google LLC |
| Firebase Realtime Database | Cloud data synchronization | Google LLC |
| Google Sign-In | Third-party authentication | Google LLC |
| Sign in with Apple | Third-party authentication | Apple Inc. |

These third-party service providers have access to your personal information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

### 5. Data Storage & Security

#### 5.1 Local Storage

- All habit records and settings are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults
- Data is isolated and protected by your unique user ID
- Local data remains on your device and is only synced when you are signed in

#### 5.2 Cloud Storage

- When you sign in, your data is securely synced to Firebase servers
- Data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- Firebase implements industry-standard security measures to protect your data

#### 5.3 Security Measures

We employ the following security practices:

- Transport layer encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission
- User ID data isolation to prevent unauthorized access
- Secure authentication mechanisms via Firebase
- Regular security reviews and updates

### 6. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We respect your privacy and commit to:

- **Not Selling Your Data:** We do not sell your personal information to third parties
- **Limited Sharing:** We do not share your personal information except as described in this policy

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **With Your Consent:** When you explicitly authorize us to share your information
- **Service Providers:** With third-party service providers (e.g., Firebase) who perform services on our behalf
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law, court order, or government regulation
- **Protection of Rights:** To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users
- **Business Transfer:** In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

### 7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

- **Access:** Request access to the personal information we hold about you
- **Correction:** Update or correct inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Data Portability:** Request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent for data processing where consent is the legal basis
- **Opt-Out:** You can opt-out of analytics by signing out or using the App in guest mode

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in Section 11.

### 8. Anonymous & Guest Mode

You can use the App without creating an account. In guest mode:

- The App generates a temporary anonymous identifier (UUID)
- Your data is stored locally on your device only
- No personal information is collected or transmitted
- You can sign in later to sync your locally stored data

### 9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

### 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The updated policy will be published within the App, and we will notify users of significant changes through appropriate channels.

Your continued use of the App after the effective date of the revised Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.
